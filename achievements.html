Achievements

All Kerala u-15 Champions 2024

In 2024, Red Star FC proudly clinched the title of All Kerala U-15 Champions, marking a significant milestone in the club's illustrious history. This victory is a testament to the dedication, hard work, and exceptional talent of our young athletes. Throughout the tournament, our U-15 team showcased remarkable skill, strategic prowess, and unwavering team spirit, overcoming formidable opponents to emerge as champions. This achievement underscores Red Star FC's commitment to nurturing young talent and fostering a culture of excellence and sportsmanship. We are immensely proud of our players and coaching staff for their relentless pursuit of victory and their embodiment of the club's values on and off the field. As we celebrate this prestigious win, we look forward to continuing our legacy of success and inspiring future generations of footballers.

Adnan selected for the Indian National Team

We are thrilled to announce that Adnan, one of Red Star FC's most promising young talents, has been selected for the Indian National Team. This prestigious selection is a significant achievement for both Adnan and our club, reflecting the hard work, dedication, and exceptional skill he has demonstrated on the field. Adnan's journey with Red Star FC has been nothing short of inspiring, and his selection to represent India at the national level is a testament to his relentless pursuit of excellence. We are incredibly proud of Adnan and confident that he will make a substantial impact on the national stage, bringing honor and recognition to our club and inspiring fellow teammates. As he embarks on this exciting new chapter, we wish him all the success and support from the entire Red Star FC community.

U-20 Team has been promoted to Division 1 in K-League

We are delighted to announce that our U-20 team has been promoted to Division 1 in the Kerala League. This remarkable achievement is the result of unwavering dedication, hard work, and the exceptional talent of our young athletes. Throughout the season, our U-20 team has showcased outstanding performance, resilience, and teamwork, earning their well-deserved spot in the top division. This promotion marks a significant milestone for Red Star FC, reflecting our commitment to nurturing young talent and striving for excellence at every level. We are incredibly proud of our U-20 players and coaching staff for their relentless efforts and determination. As we prepare for the challenges and opportunities ahead in Division 1, we look forward to continued success and growth. Congratulations to the U-20 team for this fantastic accomplishment!
